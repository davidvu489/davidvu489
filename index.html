FAQs

What is The Book Of Answers?

The Book Of Answers is a book created by Carol Bolt, The Book of Answers has the solution to all the problems of everyday life, just ask a question, open the book and you will find the answer.

What is the Book Of Answers Online?

The Book Of Answers Online is the digital version of The Book of Answers, which provides an easy way to get answers online. All the answers on this site are the same as The Book of Answers.

How do I use The Book of Answers online?

Focus on a question, close your eyes and meditate for 5 seconds, then click on the Book of Answers and it will tell you the answer.

How to use with paper book of The Book Of Answers?

If you have a paper book of The Book of Answers, simply hold the closed book in your hand and spend ten to fifteen seconds focusing on your question. While imagining or speaking your question out loud, place one palm on the cover of the book and stroke the edges of the pages from back to front. When you feel the time is right, open the page your finger touches and there is your answer! What is your answer?
